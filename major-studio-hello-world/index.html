Case Study #1

Milchrechner (Milk Calculator)

Client: Association of the German Dairy Industry

  • ~100 largest dairy cooperatives & TNCs
  • €26bn in sales
  • 31m tons of milk

Hired environmental research institute
to investigate CO² & energy footprint

Research Result: everything-final-final2.xlsx

Huge Excel file with all substantive research,
from cooling agents to logistics

Input Cells → n formulas → Output Cells

Problems:

  • Mail around and collect 100s of .xls
  • final.xlsx = Intellectual Property ©

Solution: Make it a web app

Milk Calculator: Web App

Requirements

  • Create in-memory representation of arbitrary .xls
  • Dynamically generate a multi-step form for data entry
  • Calculate output using in-memory .xls
  • Display as charts for user and save to DB
